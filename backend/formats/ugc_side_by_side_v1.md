# UGC Side-by-Side

## Blueprint

Split the canvas into a left lifestyle column (~55-60% width) and a right brand-color panel (~40-45%). Left: a full-bleed candid UGC-style photo of a real person using/wearing the product, with an oversized vertical word stack cropped off the left edge as typographic texture. Right, stacked top to bottom: a clean floating product cutout with soft shadow sitting directly on the flat brand-color background (top ~35%), then a rounded white testimonial card with a small accent tab, an italic serif quote of 3-4 short lines, and a light attribution handle beneath it, then a compact logo lockup pill with tagline pinned to the bottom-right corner. Background is a single flat brand color with sparse hand-drawn accent marks; whole right column keeps generous padding and card corner-radius consistent.

## Negatives

No star-rating clip-art, no discount badges or price starbursts, no cutesy doodles/sparkle stickers (swap for thin serif rules or nothing), no emoji in the quote, no influencer-style handle overload, no neon UGC caption stickers; quote card stays white with a single restrained accent color, photo stays natural-light candid, not staged catalog.

## Real-estate adaptation

Left column becomes a candid resident/visitor moment (family on the balcony, evening walk in the landscaped court) instead of a product selfie; the top-right product cutout becomes a small framed tower/facade render or site-plan vignette. The quote card carries a real homeowner or site-visit line ("We compared eleven projects. This was the only walk-through my wife didn't want to leave.") with a first-name + tower attribution, and the logo pill holds the project lockup + micro-location tagline.
