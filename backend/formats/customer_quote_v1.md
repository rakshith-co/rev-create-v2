# Customer Quote

## Blueprint

Single flat brand-color canvas, portrait 4:5. Left 40% is a text rail: an oversized italic pull-quote in white, opening quotation mark included, stacked one-to-two words per line filling roughly the top 60% of the frame; below it a small em-dash attribution (first name + age), a tight 5-star row, and a tiny one-line verification caption; brand logo lockup pinned bottom-left. Right 60% is a photo of a real person shot from behind on a seamless backdrop matching the background color exactly, so the image reads as one continuous surface — the product they wear/hold is the only detailed object and sits at upper-right eye level. One small sparkle/asterisk glyph floats top-left as the only ornament.

## Negatives

No starbursts, discount badges, price flashes or countdowns; no cartoon mascots or emoji; no busy lifestyle collage — one subject, one background color; subject never faces camera (the from-behind anonymity is the device); no gradient or texture on the backdrop — it must match the photo seamless exactly; quote stays under ten words, no paragraph testimonials; for premium use, swap playful rounded italic for an elegant serif italic and drop lowercase-cute styling.

## Real-estate adaptation

Replace the product-wearer with a resident or couple shot from behind — on a balcony, lawn or lobby gazing toward the tower/greens — against a deep muted-luxury field (forest green, ink navy, warm sand); the quote becomes one emotional resident line ("the kids just never come inside anymore."), the verification caption becomes "resident since 2025" or "verified homeowner, Tower B", and the stars can stay or give way to a RERA/possession micro-line above the developer logo.
