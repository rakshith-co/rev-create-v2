# Private Walk-through Invite

## Blueprint

Designed as a letterpress event invitation: cream or ink field, thin-rule border, centered serif stack — project name, 'You are invited', experience-centre address and weekend date, RSVP-style CTA ('Book your private walk-through'). One small twilight render or lifestyle vignette sits inside the rule as the only image. Reframes the Meta lead form as an appointment, which is the actual conversion event in RE; RERA microline sits outside the border at the base.
