# Location Minutes Map

## Blueprint

Minimal editorial map: flat deep-tone field with an abstracted road/metro line drawing, project pin glowing at center, 4–5 hairline routes radiating to landmark chips each stating a drive-time fact ('Airport · 22 min', 'ORR · 8 min'), no cartographic clutter. A small hero-render card sits inset in one corner tying pin to product, headline above sells the location emotionally ('Live where the city can reach you — not the reverse.'). RERA microline at the base; every minute figure must be a real, defensible number.
