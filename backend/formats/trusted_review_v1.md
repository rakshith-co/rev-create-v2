# Trusted Review

## Blueprint

Full-bleed lifestyle scene washed in a single saturated brand color; the hero subject sits centre-right across the lower 60%, angled slightly, with one colour-matched prop softly defocused behind it. Top 40%: an oversized opening quotation mark plus a 2-3 line pull-quote testimonial set in heavy condensed uppercase white, the last line flipped to the accent colour and closed with a small end-quote; a 5-star row sits directly beneath. Lower-left 45% width: a floating white rounded-corner review card overlapping the hero — circular avatar, reviewer name with a verified-badge row, 3-4 lines of small dark review copy, a grey "…Read more" link, thin divider, then a thumbs-up "N people found this helpful" footer; card casts a soft shadow and is the only white element in the frame.

## Negatives

No starbursts, discount badges, price tags or countdowns; no fake stock-photo avatars or multiple stacked review cards; keep the card UI flat and minimal (one avatar, one badge, one footer line); no rainbow gradients or neon glows — the frame stays in one brand hue plus white; headline stays a human quote, never a feature list; no more than one emoji inside the review body.

## Real-estate adaptation

Swap the staged product for a twilight tower exterior or a warm lived-in interior toned to one brand hue; the pull-quote becomes a resident's emotional line ("We stopped taking weekend getaways. Home became one.") and the card becomes a verified-homeowner / Google-review style card with the star row intact — the helpful-count footer converts to a proof stat like "4.8 from 312 resident reviews".
