# Customer Story

## Blueprint

Full-bleed top-down lifestyle photo on one single saturated color surface, hero subject placed just right of center in the upper 60% and styled with 3-4 in-world props bleeding off the frame edges. Lower-left 35% carries a social-proof stack set directly on the photo with no card or panel: a small white circle avatar glyph beside a bold lowercase @handle, an italic one-line credential underneath ("verified customer · neighborhood"), then a two-line quote in casual white handwriting script, closed by a row of five solid white stars. A tiny white date · time stamp sits in the bottom-right corner like camera metadata, selling the candid-snapshot feel. All type pure white, left-aligned, generous letter-spacing on the metadata line.

## Negatives

No review-app UI chrome or screenshot borders, no speech bubbles, no yellow clipart stars or star-rating badges in boxes, no discount stickers or price callouts, no more than one quote, handwriting confined to the quote only (never the credential or timestamp), no drop shadows or white text panels over the photo, no emoji spam beyond a single accent glyph.

## Real-estate adaptation

Swap the product flat-lay for a lived-in golden-hour frame of the project — balcony at dusk, clubhouse lawn, or a styled living room — and make the @handle a resident name with the credential line reading "resident since 2025 · Tower A" or "site-visited last month". The handwritten quote becomes one emotional line about living there ("my kids walk to school through a forest now"), stars stay as-is or become a possession/RERA microline; the timestamp keeps the candid-testimonial authenticity.
