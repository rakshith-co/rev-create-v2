# Stat Surround 2

## Blueprint

Flat warm single-tone background, portrait frame. Top 22%: a two-line, all-caps extra-bold condensed headline in the brand accent color, centered, with a one-line casual subline directly under it; a small circular rotating-text badge overlaps the headline block at the top-right edge. Middle 55%: one large hero object held/presented at center (slightly right, angled ~10°), surrounded by four benefit callouts — two stacked down the left margin, two stacked down the right margin — each callout is a thin-line circular icon, a short bold 2-3-word title, and 2 lines of tiny supporting copy, all left/right-aligned toward their margin. Bottom 15%: brand logo lockup at lower-left, and a centered small-caps social-proof strip along the bottom edge.

## Negatives

No starbursts, price flashes or discount stickers; exactly four callouts, never more; thin-line monochrome icons only — no filled cartoon icons, no emoji; keep the background one flat muted tone (no gradients, confetti or pattern fills); drop the hand-holding gimmick for premium categories — present the hero cleanly; badge stays quiet single-color, not neon.

## Real-estate adaptation

Replace the hand-held product with the tower/facade hero render (or a keyed-out building cutout) at center, and turn the four callouts into hard project stats — e.g. "5 min to metro", "80% open space", "3-side open homes", "IGBC Gold" — with the footer strip carrying social proof ("500+ families home") and the badge carrying the launch/possession tag. Headline sells the emotion, subline states the location fact casually.
