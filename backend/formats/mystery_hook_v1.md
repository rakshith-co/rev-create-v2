# Mystery Hook

## Blueprint

Off-white canvas, top ~60% is the testimonial zone: an oversized flat brand-green quotation glyph anchored in the top-left corner, with a matching closing glyph hugging the right edge at ~55% height. Left-aligned type stack inside it — a small italic setup line that withholds the payoff ("I didn't expect X to do this…"), then a 2-line ultra-bold condensed sans payoff headline in black caps at ~5x the setup size, then a short sensory subline with exactly one word italicized for emphasis, then a tiny em-dash attribution line ("– Verified Customer"). Bottom ~40% is a solid brand-green band: 4-5 product/reference shots fan edge-to-edge in a shallow overlapping arc, each slightly rotated for a casual lineup feel. Bottom-right corner of the band carries a compact trust lockup — check-in-circle icon beside a bold "TRUSTED BY THOUSANDS" title with a small sub-caption, a 5-star row beneath, and a rating + review-count line in small caps.

## Negatives

No starbursts, no discount stickers or price slashes, no clipart quote marks (glyphs must be flat, geometric, single-color), no drop shadows or gradient fills on type, no more than one accent color against black/off-white, no emoji, no fabricated review counts styled like e-commerce spam — the trust lockup stays quiet and typographic, not a badge explosion.

## Real-estate adaptation

Swap the fanned product colorways for a shallow arc of 3-4 interior/amenity renders (or one wide tower hero) on the green band, and make the quote a resident or site-visit line — italic setup "I came for the location…" paying off into a bold emotional headline. The trust lockup becomes "Trusted by 300+ families" with RERA registration or possession-date replacing the review count.
