# Progress Proof

## Blueprint

A 2×2 or split layout pairing dated real site photographs (slab count, tower crane, finished floors — each with a small date stamp) against the final render, under a bold header like 'On schedule. On record.' Footer carries possession date, RERA number and builder-legacy line. This is the trust format RE uniquely needs — de-risking an unfinished product — and no D2C-derived library has it; photos must stay honest and undramatized, no render passed off as site photo.
