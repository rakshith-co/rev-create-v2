# Lifestyle with Numbers

## Blueprint

Top 50-55%: full-bleed lifestyle/hero photo on a warm glowing gradient backdrop, subject centered with soft ground shadow; a thin micro-strip across the very top carries the brand wordmark and tagline in tiny letterspaced uppercase, separated by dot dividers. Bottom 45-50%: dark gradient panel (deep brand tone fading to near-black at the base) that blends up into the photo. On the panel, a big two-line UPPERCASE statistic headline in heavy condensed sans, set in the brand accent color, left-aligned with tight leading — the number leads the line. Under it, one short witty subline in white sentence case with 2-3 keywords tinted in the accent color; generous margins, no other elements.

## Negatives

No starbursts, price tags, discount badges or countdown clutter; no more than one statistic on the canvas; no white boxes or borders around the headline; keep the bottom panel a clean gradient — no texture, patterns or icon rows; hero photo stays photographic and uncluttered, never a collage; subline stays one line, never a paragraph.

## Real-estate adaptation

Swap the D2C product hero for a golden-hour facade, amenity or resident-lifestyle shot; the statistic becomes a trust or scarcity number ("87% of Tower A sold in 45 days", "212 families moved in", "12 mins to the airport") and the subline delivers it casually, like a friend stating a fact.
