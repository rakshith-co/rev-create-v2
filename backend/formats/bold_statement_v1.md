# Bold Statement

## Blueprint

Square canvas stacked into three bands on a warm cream base. Top ~28%: oversized bold italic serif statement headline in near-black, two lines flush left, each sentence closed with an accent-color period; small brand wordmark right-aligned in the accent color just below the headline. Middle ~50%: full-bleed lifestyle photo of a person experiencing the product in a real-world setting, shot from behind or candid, with the headline statement echoed inside the scene itself (on the product, signage or environment) as a payoff. Bottom ~22%: cream benefits panel with three equal columns separated by hairline vertical rules — each column gets a small accent-red circular line icon, a bold all-caps micro-headline, and a two-line light description — closed by a slim black footer ticker bar: 5-star row plus social-proof count on the left, short CTA phrase center, campaign hashtag right in the accent color.

## Negatives

No starbursts, price slashes or discount badges; no neon gradients or drop shadows on type; keep exactly three benefit columns — never four or a grid; icons stay thin-line monochrome accent, never multicolor emoji-style; headline stays witty-declarative serif, never all-caps shouty sans; photo stays candid editorial, no studio cut-out product on white; footer ticker stays one quiet line, no urgency timers.

## Real-estate adaptation

The lifestyle hero becomes a golden-hour shot of a resident walking toward the tower or through the landscaped deck, with the headline statement echoed on project signage or the skyline; the three benefit columns become location/amenity/trust proofs ("12 MIN TO METRO", "80% OPEN & GREEN", "RERA APPROVED"), and the footer ticker becomes "500+ families home" + "Book your private walk-through" + project hashtag.
