# Social Proof

## Blueprint

Clean white canvas split into three stacked zones. Top 25%: oversized lowercase headline in heavy black sans-serif, two left-aligned lines, one or two small inline emoji/icon accents ending the lines. Middle 30%: a rounded light-gray social-comment card — circular avatar at left, bold @handle with a muted timestamp beside it, one casual first-person testimonial line below, then an engagement row underneath the card (heart icon + like count, speech-bubble icon + reply count) in muted gray. Bottom 45%: three product hero cutouts in a row on the bare white background, evenly spaced and slightly overlapping, scaling subtly larger left to right, no props, no shadows beyond a soft contact shadow.

## Negatives

No starbursts, no discount badges, no price slashes, no five-star rating strips, no more than one comment card, no emoji pile-ups in the quote (max one), no neon or gradient backgrounds — keep the canvas pure white with generous breathing room; the comment must read like a real person, never ad-speak ("world-class amenities" is banned inside the quote).

## Real-estate adaptation

Replace the sneaker trio with three property frames — exterior hero, a signature interior, and an amenity/view angle — and make the comment card a resident or recent-buyer quote told like a friend ("moved in last month, still not over the sunrise from the 22nd floor"). Keep the headline emotional and lowercase but drop the emoji stack to hold a premium register.
