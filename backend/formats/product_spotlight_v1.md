# Product Spotlight

## Blueprint

4:5 canvas in three stacked bands on a single deep saturated field (navy). Top ~35%: centered all-caps serif headline in warm cream, stacked over three tight lines, with a small thin-outline pill CTA in the same cream directly beneath. Middle ~45%: the hero cutout sits large and slightly tilted on a diagonal color-block podium — two or three angled planes in one contrasting accent color slicing across the band — with a hard studio shadow grounding it; the navy field continues behind so the podium reads as a stage. Bottom ~20%: cream footer strip with a row of three icon+label benefit chips separated by hairline vertical dividers (tiny line icons, letterspaced caps micro-labels), and a centered letterspaced brand wordmark on the very bottom edge.

## Negatives

No starbursts, price flashes or discount badges; no cartoon mascots or sticker graphics on the hero; podium planes stay tonal and architectural (stone, terracotta, muted bronze), never candy-bright clashing color; max three benefit chips, no icon clutter; serif headline stays cream-on-dark with tight leading, no drop shadows or outlines on type; keep the field a single deep color, no gradients or bokeh.

## Real-estate adaptation

Swap the product cutout for a cutout tower/facade render staged on a muted architectural plinth (stone or dusk-terracotta planes) against a deep evening-navy field; the three-chip footer becomes the spec trio told plainly — e.g. "12 min to airport / 5-acre clubhouse / 3 & 4 BHK" — with the emotion-led headline on top and a "Book your private walk-through" pill CTA.
