# EOI Window (Premium Urgency)

## Blueprint

Flat deep field, centered serif date lockup as the hero of the frame — 'EOI closes 14 July' — with a thin horizontal allocation bar beneath it stating the scarcity as fact, not fear ('42 of 180 homes allocated'). The tower render sits in the lower third, one restrained circular launch seal, developer lockup and RERA strip at the base. Urgency carried entirely by typography and a true number: no countdown clocks, no red, no starbursts.
