# Floor-Plan Tease

## Blueprint

A blueprint-toned floor plan fills ~60% of the canvas but is deliberately cropped at one edge; a single zone (the deck, master suite or living span) is tint-highlighted with one hairline callout stating the sqft or dimension fact told casually ('the deck alone is 14 ft deep'). Emotional headline top ('Bigger than your first apartment. Just the balcony.'), CTA line offers the full plan via the lead form — the crop is the lead-gen mechanic. Fine-line draughtsman style only, no cartoon icons, RERA + config microline in the footer.
