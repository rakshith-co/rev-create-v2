# Highlighted Hook

## Blueprint

Split the canvas roughly 55/45: left column carries the story, right column is a full-height product hero standing on the baseline, cropped slightly at the right edge. Top-left corner: small circular reviewer avatar with name, a verified-buyer tick and a 5-star row beneath it. Under that, a 6-8 line first-person testimonial quote in dark mixed-weight type, with 2-3 key phrases swiped in a marker-highlight color block so the hook reads even on skim. Bottom-left: a compact trust strip — icon plus "TRUSTED BY THOUSANDS" micro-headline and a one-line subline — and a small circular logo badge pinned in the corner. Background is a warm soft-gradient scene wash with a few ingredient/context props scattered at the corners and light hand-drawn doodle accents; everything stays behind the text column.

## Negatives

No starbursts, no discount badges or price slashes, no neon-yellow highlighter or cartoon doodles — highlights become a muted champagne/warm-sand swipe or a fine underline. No fruit/ice props or busy scatter; background is a single calm architectural or dusk-sky wash. Avatar must look like a real person, not stock-smile; keep the star row small and quiet, never gold-glitter.

## Real-estate adaptation

The right-column product hero becomes a vertical crop of the tower facade or a signature interior; the testimonial becomes a resident/buyer quote with the emotional phrase and one hard fact (location, view, possession) highlighted. The trust strip becomes "Trusted by 400+ families" or a RERA/possession line, and the corner badge carries the developer logo.
