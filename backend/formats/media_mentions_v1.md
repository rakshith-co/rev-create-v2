# Media Mentions

## Blueprint

Deep warm-brown backdrop with a soft amber spotlight vignette centered behind the product. Top 12% is a press bar: a tiny uppercase eyebrow ("AS FEATURED IN") above a single row of 4-5 publication wordmarks in mixed editorial logotypes, separated by small dot bullets. Middle 30% carries an oversized italic serif pull-quote in cream, wrapped in large quotation marks across 2-3 centered lines, with a small letterspaced attribution line ("— NAME, TITLE") beneath it. Bottom half is the physical hero: the product staged center on tactile props (stack of vintage books on a trunk), lit by the warm glow so it reads like a magazine still-life; no other text below.

## Negatives

No starbursts, price tags, discount badges or CTA buttons; no more than one quote; publication row stays small and quiet (no giant logos); quote must sound like an editor wrote it, not ad copy; keep the palette to two warm tones plus cream type — no neon accents, no gradients fighting the spotlight, no cluttered props that upstage the hero.

## Real-estate adaptation

Swap the staged product for a moody golden-hour facade or clubhouse still of the project on the lower half, keep the spotlight treatment. Press row becomes real coverage (ET Realty, Times Property, Livemint, local city press) and the pull-quote becomes an actual editor/press line or an awards-jury citation about the project — trust-by-third-party is exactly how ₹1Cr+ buyers de-risk a builder.
